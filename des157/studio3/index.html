Game of PIG

Rules

  1. First player is chosen randomly.
  2. The player whose turn it is rolls the dice.
  3. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one". If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other playerâ€™s turn.
  4. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a "one" was not rolled) or they can pass to the other player.
    • WARNING: If you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out.
  5. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.
